About me.

I’m a product designer with
more than 9 years of experience
in a variety of domains.

Storytelling has always been at the heart of everything I do. Growing up in Hyderabad, I was captivated by cartoons and sketching—early passions that naturally led me to pursue six years of formal education in animation.

I studied animation at JNTU, Hyderabad, and later earned a Master of Fine Arts from SCAD, Atlanta, where I focused on illustration, visual design, and conceptual art. During this time, I collaborated on short films, refining my ability to craft compelling visual narratives.

My career began as a visual designer at a Houston-based startup, where I immersed myself in user-centered design. What started as a visual design role quickly evolved into product design as I embraced problem-solving and scalable design thinking.

Over the years I’ve worked across multiple startups, building impactful digital products and modular design systems. My structured mindset naturally led me to design systems where I’ve been able to create scalable processes that drive efficiency.

One of my most notable achievements was leading the development of the Sense Design System at Jupiter Money, which streamlined workflows for 50+ stakeholders and improved efficiency by 400% in 18 months.

Beyond design, I’m an avid athlete with a deep passion for boxing and long-distance running. I’ve competed in professional boxing matches and continue to train with a disciplined approach.

I thrive at the intersection of storytelling, structured design thinking, and pushing boundaries—whether in product design, sports, or making a difference for animals.
